Introduction

Welcome! If you're reading this, it's likely because I've already reached out to you. My name is Sean (blue jumper), and I run SX4.

I’m a passionate web developer who specializes in creating simple yet effective brochure websites for local businesses. My focus is on helping small businesses establish an online presence without breaking the bank. By using efficient platforms and streamlined processes, I’m able to keep costs low and quality high.

Brochure Websites

Think of a brochure website as your business card on the web—simple, sleek, and effective. These lightweight websites are perfect for local businesses that want a professional online presence without complexity.

In a world where consumers are returning to favoring websites over apps and social media profiles, having a website is more important than ever. In fact, a recent survey found that 84% of consumers believe a business with a website is more credible than one with only a social media profile.
