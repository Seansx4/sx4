Website Design

Custom Websites for Small Local Businesses

We specialize in creating custom-coded websites tailored for small local businesses. Our approach focuses on building lightweight brochure websites that help establish an online presence without the complexity or expense associated with large platforms like WordPress, Wix or SquareSpace. By opting for a SX4, you avoid ongoing monthly fees and receive a cost-effective solution to meet your business needs.

Brochure websites allow businesses to showcase key information, testimonials, and updates, making them an ideal solution for businesses that rely on local customer engagement. With the majority of consumers researching businesses online before visiting in person, having a professional online presence is crucial.

Why Choose a Brochure Website?

A brochure website provides your business with a strong online presence, allowing you to showcase key details such as services, pricing, and contact information. These sites are perfect for businesses that don’t require complex databases but still want to maintain a professional image.

According to a recent survey, 76% of consumers look at a business's online presence before visiting in person, and 84% of consumers believe that a business with a website is more credible than one that only has a social media profile.

Our custom-coded brochure websites ensure your business appears credible and accessible to potential customers, making them an excellent choice for local customer engagement.

Why choose SX4?

No Monthly Fees

Many websites are built using website builders like WordPress or Wix, which come with ongoing subscription costs. We eliminate those fees by creating websites from scratch, providing you with a fully functional, secure website without the burden of monthly charges.

All of our websites include a free SSL security certificate, which enhances your site's security and is favored by search engines like Google. This can save you an additional €80-130 per year compared to other platforms.

Our Approach

Our unique approach involves fully creating a website before engaging in extended design discussions. This streamlined process saves both time and money for our clients, focusing on getting your business online as quickly as possible.

Once your website is live, we can work on refining the design to meet your specific needs, ensuring that your online presence is effective and user-friendly.

Google Tools

All of our websites come with pre-installed Google Analytics and Google Search Console. These tools help you monitor website traffic and optimize your site's visibility on search engines, ensuring that your website performs at its best. Additionally, we provide monthly reports to keep you informed about your website's performance.
